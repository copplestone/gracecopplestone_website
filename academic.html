Institute for Manufacturing, University of Cambridge

At Cambridge I read general engineering for two years and specialised in manufacturing engineering for two years. I received a combined Bachelors and Masters in Manufacturing Engineering from the Institute for Manufacturing.

  • Master's thesis - An Investigation into Sustainability in the Aerospace and Automotive Supply Networks in the UK and India - In this work I interviewed stakeholders in different tiers of the automotive and aerospace supply networks to determine the state of sustainable manufacturing practice.

  • Industrial experience - Completed several on-site industrial projects including process improvement projects in a sandwich and automotive component factory as well an R&D project at the Cadbury's chocolate factory.

  • Extra-curricular - Committee member of Cambridge University Technology and Enterprise Club, competed for the university athletics and cross country club, member of JUNK percussion band.
